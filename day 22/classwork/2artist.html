Michelangelo

Michelangelo was a Renaissance master. He created the David sculpture and painted the Sistine Chapel ceiling. He believed art existed within the stone itself.

Andy Warhol

Andy Warhol was a Pop Art icon. His art turned everyday items like soup cans into cultural statements. He blurred the lines between fame, fashion, and creativity.
