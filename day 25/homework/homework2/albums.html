More Albums by Laufey

  • Album - "Bewitched" (2023)

    "Bewitched" is Laufey's critically acclaimed debut album. This album blends jazz, classical, and indie pop elements to create a timeless and captivating sound. Featuring lush orchestral arrangements and Laufey's hauntingly beautiful voice, "Bewitched" showcases her deep love for storytelling through music.

    The album explores themes of love, longing, and nostalgia, with tracks that touch on personal reflections and romantic dreams. The title track, "Bewitched," captures the feeling of being enchanted by someone special, while songs like "Candlelight" and "Ivy" take listeners on a journey of introspection.

  • Album - "About Love" (2022)

    "About Love" is a deeply emotional and intimate album that explores the complexity of human emotions in the context of love and relationships. Known for her delicate yet powerful voice, Laufey invites listeners to experience the beauty and pain that often accompanies love.

    The album features romantic ballads, with songs like "Love You Like That" and "Falling Into You" drawing upon her classical training and jazz influences. "About Love" is a perfect combination of poetic lyricism and soulful melodies that resonate deeply with those who have ever experienced the highs and lows of love.
