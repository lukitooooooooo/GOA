black mirror

Black Mirror bis a British anthology TV series created by Charlie Brooker. It first premiered in 2011 and has become known for its dark, thought-provoking stories about the relationship between humans and technology.

Each episode is standalone (like a mini-movie), often set in a dystopian near future. The show explores themes like:

  • Surveillance and privacy
  • Artificial intelligence
  • Social media addiction
  • Virtual reality
  • The dark side of technological progress
