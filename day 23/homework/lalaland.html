La la land

La La Land is a romantic musical film released in 2016, written and directed by Damien Chazelle. It stars Ryan Gosling as Sebastian (a jazz musician) and Emma Stone as Mia (an aspiring actress), and it's all about dreams, love, and the cost of chasing your passion in Los Angeles

What the Film Is About

  • Dreams and Ambitions
  • Love Story
  • Art vs Reality
  • Bittersweet Ending
