About Facebook

History

Facebook was founded in 2004 by Mark Zuckerberg and his college roommates at Harvard University. It started as a platform for college students and later opened up to everyone.

Founders

Mark Zuckerberg, Eduardo Saverin, Andrew McCollum, Dustin Moskovitz, and Chris Hughes are the co-founders of Facebook.

Growth

Today, Facebook has over 2.8 billion monthly active users and owns other major platforms like Instagram and WhatsApp.
